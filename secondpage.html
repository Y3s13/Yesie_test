SEWING

How I got into sewing

I was 7-9 years old when I started to sew, I watched people on YouTube people sewing all the time. The first thing I sewed was when I was fixing my rainbow dash slippers, I was sewing back the wings that fell out

Timeline

Age 7-8: I've fixed the wings of my rainbow dash slippers

Age 9-10:I used my mom's and mine clothes that didn't fit me to sew, I mostly made small bags, I had hand sew for the majority of my sewing experience

Age 11-12: I quit sewing for a while, There is no specific reason why I quit sewing for a while but i did

Age 13-14: I started to sew again and my mom bought me a sewing machine, and I became the family sewer. To be specific non-complicated stuff to fix (ex: putting a bias on the curtain, fixing a rip from shirt)
